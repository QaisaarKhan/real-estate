Always Do Your Research
Be clear on the current status of the market. Through your different methods of research, be sure to find out about the status of the property and location.

Interact with investors
As part of your research, speak to investors about how they invested in growing markets. They can tell you firsthand about the current situation of the markets in your desired location.

Location is key
Buy property in an area, which is expected to show strong growth in the near future. In real estate, "Where" is more important than "Where", so choose your location wisely for the best return on your investment.

Budgeting plans
Before investing, make sure that you have completed a detailed budgeting plan of your income and expenses to avoid negative gearing of your property.

Measure demand realistically
Demand only determines the value of real estate and a wise investment decision is based on realistic estimates. Property purchased for investment, be it long-term or short-term, will yield better gains only if the future development is sound.
